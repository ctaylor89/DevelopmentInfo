QuickBooks Task Practice	For practice and quick lookup
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Paying the bill is recorded separately to receiving the bill
Entering a bill records the liability (A/P). Paying the bill clears that liability.
These are always two separate transactions in QBO.
Bills are specifically for delayed payment.
If the client pays immediately, you use an expense or check, not a bill.
---
Use the Transfer feature to move the transaction from the bank account to the credit card account.
Or
Use the “Pay down credit card” feature
Both of these are correct because they do the same thing under the hood — they properly move money from the bank account to the 
credit card liability account, keeping your books clean and reconcilable.
